## Key Changes Summary

### Bonus Types Now Offered:
1. **Guaranteed "Win or Lose"**: Underdog, PrizePicks
   - Users get bonus regardless of outcome
   - Best for user confidence/retention

2. **Conditional (First Bet Wins)**: FanDuel
   - Requires winning first bet to get bonus
   - Slightly higher risk for users but still valuable

3. **Risk-Free Entry**: Boom Fantasy
   - First entry is free, bonus if you lose
   - Good for no-risk-of-loss positioning

4. **Match Bonuses**: Dabble
   - Direct 1:1 match on deposit
   - Clear value proposition

5. **Ticket + Prize Entry**: DraftKings
   - Contest ticket + entry to massive prize pools
   - Emphasizes prize pool upside

6. **Direct Cash Bonuses**: Parlay Play, BETR, OwnersBox, Sleeper (Free)
   - Straightforward bonus structures
   - Easy to communicate

### Strategic Positioning:
- **Highest Value Bonus**: OwnersBox ($200)
- **Most Attractive Terms**: PrizePicks & Underdog (win or lose guaranteed)
- **Best for Beginners**: Boom Fantasy (risk-free) & Dabble (low minimum)
- **Most Established**: DraftKings & FanDuel
- **Best Value**: Sleeper (Free to play, no bonus needed)
